# DCF-Valuation-Model
Valuation &amp; Modeling: Engineered a comprehensive 3-Statement Operating Model and derived an equity valuation using DCF methodology. Conducted Sensitivity Analysis to quantify downside risk and determine a 25% margin of safety for M&amp;A scenarios.


---

## 🏛️ Valuation Architecture & Capital Structure Modeling

A dynamic, transparent corporate finance environment built to evaluate corporate capital structures, project multi-scenario cash flow conversions, and determine intrinsic asset value.

### 1. Free Cash Flow (FCF) Conversion Loops
The model bypasses standard accounting net income to isolate true economic reality by engineering a rigorous Unlevered Free Cash Flow (FCFF) pipeline:

* **NOPAT:** EBIT adjusted for structural tax rates to calculate baseline operating profitability.
* **Working Capital Dynamics:** Integrates Net Working Capital adjustments, tracking operational liquidity conversion efficiency.
* **Capital Discipline:** Factors in Capital Expenditures (CapEx) required to sustain structural revenue growth layers.

### 2. Multi-Scenario Sensitivity Stress Testing
To insulate the valuation thesis against macro shocks, the model incorporates dynamic sensitivity grids:
* **WACC & Terminal Growth Matrix:** Cross-references varying Weighted Average Cost of Capital parameters against terminal growth rates to establish a robust margin-of-safety range.
* **Revenue Degradation Models:** Simulates margin compression cycles to track structural cash flow persistence under downside economic scenarios.
